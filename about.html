Our Story

From Sembabule to Serving Uganda

Founded in May 2023, Apagan Investments Ug was born from a simple observation: Ugandan businesses, especially small-scale manufacturers and retailers, were struggling with unreliable suppliers, inflated prices, and fragmented supply chains.

We set out to change that. Starting from our headquarters on Mateete Road in Sembabule, we built a procurement operation focused on what matters most — reliability, competitive pricing, and speed. Within our first year, we expanded to our main branch in Kampala's 2nd Street Industrial Area to be closer to our growing client base.

Today, Apagan Investments Ug serves over 250 active clients across manufacturing, construction, agriculture, retail, and more. We've fulfilled over 5,000 orders and continue to grow — but our commitment to honest, dependable trading remains unchanged.
